How does it work?

Our app allows you to enter information about your Airbnb rental property. Then, using historical Airbnb data, it generates the best price to charge for your rental. You'll always know the best price to make you the most money.

Data Points

Get the best price based on:

  • Bedrooms
  • Beds
  • Bathrooms
  • Amenities
  • Guest Allowed

Account Features

When you sign up for an account, you will have the ability to add, edit, and delete listing. Made some changes to your rental property? Get upgrade prices when you edit your lisitings. Click here to sign up and get started!
